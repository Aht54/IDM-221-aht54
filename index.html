Hello! I`m Anthony and I am an aspiring UXID designer

Here you will be able to get a sense of who I am as a person and what ignites my passions in life.

Passions

As I`ve gotten older, my definition of life has been constantly changing at an everyday pace. Along the way, these 3 things have been the most recent passions that I`ve recently discovered by getting out of my comfort zone. The one main lesson that I have learned from each of these passions is that anything new that you decide to take on, will always be a struggle initally but over time, it can be polished to a very useful skills I`ve personally symbolized.

Photography

Attention

I was always drawn to Photography in a way where I was afraid from making the transition to a higher level because of how addicted it could be. Turns out... I was right! Now I almost always have my camera on me and ready to capture anything that catches my eye.

Volleyball

Reliance

I started playing Volleyball in High School and it was one of the best decisions I`ve ever made. The sport taught me how to build connections and emphasized the critical importance of an team environment. Growing up I had a hard time opening up to people and had a constant sense of self-reliance which I`m glad to have grown out of.

Drawing

Patience

When I saw a drawing course on my schedule for the fall term, I was honestly not looking forward to it at all because of the thought of overwhelming embarassment. I`ve never been a fan of drawing nor had the talent but over the course of this term, I now realize that I just had to give the process a chance. Looking back at my struggles in the beginning and comparing it to the results now, I`m proud of what I was able to accomplish.
